# IT Consulting Proposal

## Overview
Generate structured IT consulting proposals for enterprise clients.
Cover the full lifecycle: understand client needs → map to service areas →
propose collaboration model → present case studies → define next steps.

## When to trigger
- User wants to write a proposal or pitch for an IT consulting engagement
- User needs a capability document or service overview for a client meeting
- User wants to structure a SOW (Statement of Work)
- User asks to create a "proposta", "pitch", "apresentação de serviços"

## Workflow

### Step 1 — Gather context
Ask (or infer):
- **Client industry**: banking, retail, pharma, telecom, public sector, etc.
- **Service area needed**: UX, apps, integration, automation, AI, cloud, QA?
- **Engagement type**: new project, team augmentation, ongoing partnership?
- **Client maturity**: greenfield, modernization, optimization?
- **Language**: Portuguese (PT) or English?

### Step 3 — Collaboration models

Present the appropriate model(s) based on the client need:

| Model | Description | Best for |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| **Team Augmentation** | Embeds the client's technical team with experts, strategic ownership stays with the client | Long-term technical capacity needs |
| **Fixed Scope Projects** | Clearly defined delivery with predictable outcomes, limited to a predefined scope | Well-defined projects with clear requirements |
| **CX as a Service** | Multidisciplinary team working continuously, owning strategic goals and delivering long-term value | Ongoing product evolution |
| **Hourly Packages** | Combines expert consultants with responsive support, designed to evolve with the business | Flexible support and advisory |

### Step 4 — Proposal structure

```markdown
# Proposta — [Client Name / Project Name]

## Contexto
[1-2 sentences: who is the client and what they are trying to achieve]

## Desafio
[Bullet list of the main challenges / pain points the client faces]

## Abordagem Proposta
[How the engagement will be structured — phases, methodology, team]

## Serviços e Competências
[List the relevant service areas with brief description of what will be delivered]

### [Service Area 1]
[What will be done, technologies]

### [Service Area 2]
[What will be done, technologies]

## Modelo de Colaboração
[Recommended collaboration model with justification]

## Âmbito
[What is included — and optionally what is out of scope]

## Entregáveis
[List of deliverables]

## Próximos Passos
1. [Action 1]
2. [Action 2]
3. [Action 3]
```

## Key principles
- Lead with business value, not technology
- Match the proposal language to the client's maturity (technical vs. executive audience)
- Always propose a clear MVP or Phase 1 — avoid overwhelming the client
- Reference relevant partnerships and awards when they strengthen credibility
- Keep the tone professional but direct — avoid buzzword-heavy filler
- Write in the client's language (PT or EN) unless told otherwise
