# Brand Guidelines

## Overview
Applies Anthropic's official brand colors and typography to artifacts.

## Anthropic Brand Tokens
- Primary: #D4A843 (gold), #1A1A1A (near-black), #F5F0E8 (cream)
- Font: Tiempos Headline (headings), Söhne (body)
- Tone: calm, precise, thoughtful

## When to trigger
- User asks for Anthropic-branded visuals, slides, or documents
- Brand colors or style standards are mentioned

## Rules
- Never invent brand colors — use only documented tokens
- Maintain contrast ratios for accessibility (WCAG AA minimum)
